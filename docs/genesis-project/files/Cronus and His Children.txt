### Core Myth (precise form)
Cronus, a Titan and ruler of the cosmos before the Olympians, was told by prophecy that one of his children would overthrow him. To prevent this, he **swallowed each child at birth**.

### Children involved
The children he consumed were:
- Hestia  
- Demeter  
- Hera  
- Hades  
- Poseidon  

His wife, **Rhea**, eventually intervened:
- She hid their youngest child, **Zeus**
- She gave Cronus a **stone wrapped in cloth**, which he swallowed instead

### Resolution
When Zeus matured, he forced Cronus to **regurgitate his siblings**, and then led the Olympians in overthrowing him (the Titanomachy).

# IV. Top-level layers

## 1. `apps/` — control plane

```text
apps/
├── gateway-api/
├── operator-console/
├── tenant-admin-console/
├── reporting-dashboard/
├── research-console/
└── public-docs-site/
```

`gateway-api/` is the system edge. It receives asset, campaign, experiment, report, and administrative requests.  
`operator-console/` is the internal operating surface for campaign and workflow supervision.  
`tenant-admin-console/` is the SaaS administration surface for workspace, brand, access, and billing context.  
`reporting-dashboard/` is the human-readable output layer for asset yield, audience transitions, ROI, and platform deltas.  
`research-console/` is the runtime epistemic surface for hypotheses, evidence, experiments, and platform-state memos.  
`public-docs-site/` is for product and implementation documentation.

This split matches the control-plane / execution-plane distinction in the operator-grade and SaaS documents. fileciteturn1file1 fileciteturn1file15

